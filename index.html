Questions

How it works

Is my code confidential?

Yes. I'll sign your NDA before you share anything, and access is removed when the review is delivered. Nothing from your codebase is ever reused or disclosed.

Do we need to get on a call?

No. The whole process is async and written — intake form, the report, and follow-up by message. That keeps it fast and works across time zones. A call is optional, never required.

How do I pay, and when?

Fixed price agreed up front. Invoice in USD (bank transfer / Wise). Typically 50% to start and 50% on delivery for first-time clients; full upfront for the Focused tier.

What if you don't find much?

Good — that's a signal in itself. You still get a written report documenting what was reviewed, the standards checked, and a clear statement of the firmware's risk posture you can share with stakeholders or auditors.

Which languages / standards do you cover?

Embedded C (and C++). MISRA C:2012, CWE/security, and a functional-safety lens for ISO 26262 / IEC 61508 / IEC 62304 on the Deep tier. MCUs: STM32, ESP32, Microchip, 8051, and others.
